FAQ

The best way to book is to contact me directly through the form on my website. Please include details like the type of shoot you're looking for, your preferred dates, and any specific ideas you have. I'll get back to you within 24-48 hours.

My pricing varies depending on the type of shoot, location, and hours required. Please visit my Pricing page for detailed package information, or contact me for a custom quote.

Yes, a non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking. This ensures that your date is reserved and allows me to begin preparing for your session.

I recommend wearing comfortable clothing that reflects your personality. For group or family photos, it's often a good idea to coordinate colors without being too "matchy." Once your session is booked, I'll send you a more detailed guide with tips on clothing, colors, and accessories.

Most standard portrait or family sessions last between 1 to 2 hours. This gives us enough time to capture a variety of shots in different poses and locations.

If the weather is unsuitable (e.g., heavy rain, strong winds), we will reschedule the session to a mutually agreed-upon date at no extra cost.

You can expect to receive your online proofing gallery within 1-2 weeks after the photoshoot. Once you've made your final selections, the fully edited high-resolution digital files will be delivered within an additional 1-2 weeks.

The number of final photos depends on the package you choose. You can find the exact number of included images in the package details on my Pricing page. You will have the option to purchase additional photos from your gallery if you wish.

I do not provide unedited or raw files. My style is defined by my post-production and editing process, and the final edited images represent my finished work.

Yes, you will receive a print release with your digital gallery, which allows you to print the photos for personal use. I recommend using a professional lab for the best quality prints.
